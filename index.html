Farming With Care: More Than a Business

We produced "Farming With Care: More Than a Business". This was my first experience as a producer, and it was a fantastic learning experience. This documentary is about a Care Farm in the Netherlands, owned by my family members, where people with an intellectual and/or physical disability and/or psychiatric disorder are offered appropriate and valuable daytime activities.
