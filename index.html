Farming With Care: More Than a Business

In my second year of my Bachelor, I had the privilege to produce a personal short-documentary.
Together with my amazing team, we produced
"Farming With Care: More Than a Business".
This documentary is about a Care Farm in the Netherlands, owned by my family members. The Care Farm is a small-scale farm where people with an intellectual and/or physical disability and/or psychiatric disorder are offered appropriate and valuable daytime activities.

This was my first experience with producing, and it was a fantastic learning experience.
